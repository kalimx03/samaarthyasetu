The Problem

India has 40 million+ persons
with disabilities.
Only 15% are formally employed.

01

Zero Job Connect

NGOs like Samarthanam train hundreds yearly in real skills — IT, tailoring, bakery. After training ends, candidates fall into a black hole. No matching, no employer connect, no follow-up.

02

Scheme Maze

21 disability certificate types. 15+ government schemes. Families need a lawyer to understand what they're entitled to. No platform explains it in Kannada or Hindi.

03

Zero Accountability

Government tracks training completions, not employment. NGOs mark candidates as "placed" before offers confirm. Employers have no public inclusivity score to be held to.

04

Invisible Data

Ward-level placement data doesn't exist. No one knows which neighborhoods have trained candidates, which employers are hiring, or where interventions are needed most.

How It Works

Four pillars.
One mission.

01

Verified
Skills Profile

UDID-linked identity. Disability type, skills, language preference, and accommodation needs in one structured record. NGO training history auto-attached. Ward-level indexing for hyperlocal matching.

02

AI Job
Matching

PyTorch Siamese network scores every candidate-job pair across skill overlap (60%), accommodation fit (25%), and language compatibility (15%). Top 3 curated matches surfaced per candidate.

03

Scheme
Navigator

Step-by-step wizard for 10+ government schemes. Instant eligibility check by disability certificate type. Prioritized document checklist in Kannada, Hindi, and English.

04

Live
Dashboard

Ward-level hiring outcomes — not projections, not training completions. Actual jobs, updated weekly. NGO scores, employer accountability ratings, and conflict resolution when records disagree.

Weighted composite scoring

Every candidate-job pair is scored by a PyTorch Siamese network trained on realistic Bangalore data. It models whether the employer's accommodations actually cover the candidate's specific needs — not keyword matching.

    SamaarthyaSetuEnvironment, Action
)

# Task 3 (Hard) — Placement Reconciliation
env = SamaarthyaSetuEnvironment("task_003")
obs = env.reset()

actions = [
  Action(action_type="get_candidate",
         parameters={"candidate_id":"cand_011"}),
  Action(action_type="resolve_placement_conflict",
         parameters={
           "conflict_type":"ngo_employer_mismatch",
           "resolution":"schedule_fresh_interview"
         }),
  Action(action_type="schedule_interview",
         parameters={"employer_id":"emp_001"}),
  Action(action_type="publish_dashboard_update",
         parameters={"ward":"Malleshwaram"}),
  Action(action_type="finalize_task", parameters={}),
]

for action in actions:
    obs, reward, done, info = env.step(action)
    if done: break

PyTorch Model

Siamese skill
matching network.

Dual encoder → compatibility head

Candidate and job features are encoded independently by a shared encoder. Embeddings are concatenated and scored by a compatibility head. Trained on 150 candidate-job pairs using rule-based match scores as ground truth. Loss: MSE. Optimizer: AdamW + CosineAnnealing.
